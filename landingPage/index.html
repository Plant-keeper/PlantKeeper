PlantKeeper

PlantKeeper is the perfect companion for plant enthusiasts, offering a seamless and intelligent way to ensure your plants thrive.

Features

Sensor Integration

Place sensors in your plant pots to continuously monitor humidity, UV light, and temperature in real-time, ensuring optimal plant health.

Plant monitoring dashboard

Easily view detailed data on each plant’s humidity, solar exposure, and temperature, all in an intuitive, easy-to-read format.

Automated notifications

Receive email alerts when your plants need watering or have had enough sunlight, ensuring optimal care without constant monitoring.

Plant information database

Access a comprehensive database of plant care tips and insights directly within the app, tailored to different plant types for informed care decisions.

User-friendly

Easily create a profile, add sensors, and monitor your plants through an intuitive web app, with options to select plant types and link them to specific sensors for personalized care.
